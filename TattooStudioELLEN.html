ELLEN

ellen came to malta by way of canada, bringing with her a deep love for all things traditional — not just in tattoos, but in art, cinema, and culture. her roots are firmly planted in classic american traditional work, but over time she’s expanded into neo-traditionalism, folk styles, and illustrative linework, always with a keen eye for harmony and balance. bold lines, rich symbolism, and clean storytelling are her signatures. her work nods to the past, but always hits in the present.

availability: on walk-in fridays or a few weeks in advance

price range: €100-€120p/h
