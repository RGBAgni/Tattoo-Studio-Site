OUR STORY

kismet ink was founded in 2022 by naila, a realism artist with a vision: tattoos that go deeper than skin. it started as a one-chair setup and quickly grew into a home for like-minded creatives. ellen joined in 2023 with her bold traditional style, emilio in 2024, a piercing visionnaire, and mari came on six months ago — a fine arts student with a quiet vibe and loud work. we’re a crew of artists, storytellers, and rebels, and this is where it all connects.
