NAILA

naila opened kismet ink in 2022 after years of grinding in the industry. she’s been tattooing for 7 years, specializing in bold, detailed realism that hits hard and holds up. portraits, animals, surreal moments — if it looks like a photo, she probably did it. before opening the studio, she trained under world-renowned tattooists in london and amsterdam and even osaka, japan! when she’s not tattooing, she’s sketching or blasting music in the studio. kismet is her space to create, connect, and keep it real.

availability: walk in fridays or book 2 months in advance

price range: €120-€150p/h
